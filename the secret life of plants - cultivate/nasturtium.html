Garden Nasturtium

Tropaeolum majus


Containers, garden beds, little corners… I don’t need much. Give me a planter of my own, and I’m perfectly happy.


Coleus hogs the spotlight, but gardeners know me as the good neighbor — I keep pests busy so everyone else can breathe.


Summer heat is exhausting. I’d rather wait for cooler days before I show off my flowers again.
