Wisteria

Wisteria sinensis


Why stay grounded when I can climb? Give me something to hold onto, and I’ll twirl my way up into the spotlight.


Red Yucca keeps to itself down there, and that’s fine by me. I do my own thing up here, making sure pollinators don’t miss my flowers.


Late May to June? That’s my main act. But if the summer or fall warms up, I might just surprise you with a few more blooms. Frost? Nope…not my style.
